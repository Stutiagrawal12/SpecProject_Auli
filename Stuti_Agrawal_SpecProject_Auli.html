01

Brand Audit — Where Auli Is Now

Auli Lifestyle is a Chandigarh-based Ayurvedic skincare brand founded in 2018. Products are formulated around traditional Indian botanical ingredients — Kumkumadi, Neem, Turmeric — with a premium, clean-beauty positioning. The brand has genuine product credibility but its social presence underdelivers compared to that story.

What's working
  • Strong ingredient story — Ayurveda angle is credible and differentiated
  • Product packaging is aesthetic and photogenic
  • Growing D2C skincare category tailwind in India
  • Loyal repeat customer base with real reviews
Gaps on social
  • Content is product-heavy — too many flat lays, not enough storytelling
  • No consistent content voice or recurring formats
  • X presence is minimal — missing a conversation-led audience
  • No behind-the-scenes or founder content building trust
  • User-generated content not being amplified
Opportunity
The "slow beauty" movement is gaining traction among Indian Gen Z — less products, more intention. Auli's Ayurvedic roots are a perfect fit for this shift. The brand needs to sell a ritual, not just a product.
Competitive gap
  • Minimalist.in — strong educational content, weak aesthetic
  • Juicy Chemistry — good storytelling, crowded positioning
  • Forest Essentials — premium but cold, no community feel
  • Auli's whitespace: warm, ritual-driven, deeply Indian but modern
02

Audience Persona

R
Rhea
24 · Delhi / Bangalore
Who she is
Working professional or final-year student. Spends ₹1,500–4,000/month on skincare. Reads ingredient labels. Distrustful of mass-market brands.
What she wants
Skincare that feels intentional. Brands that educate, not just sell. Content that fits into her aesthetic. Morning routine as self-care ritual.
Where she is
Instagram Reels (saves tutorials), X (skincare discourse, hot takes), Pinterest (mood boards, routine inspo).
What stops her buying
Doesn't trust brands that only show polished ads. Needs social proof, real results, and someone who "gets" Indian skin.
Her trigger
"If it feels like it was made for me specifically — my skin type, my climate, my culture — I'll buy it."
Content she saves
Ingredient explainers, "what I use every morning," honest reviews, Ayurvedic tips, slow living aesthetics.
03

Content Pillars

Every post maps to one of four pillars. The mix keeps the feed from feeling like a catalogue while ensuring each piece has a clear job.

🌿
The Ritual
Morning and evening routines. Slow beauty content. "How to use" in an aspirational, non-instructional way. Makes the product feel like a practice, not a purchase.
35% of content
🧪
The Ingredient
Deep dives into Kumkumadi, Neem, Saffron, Turmeric. "Why we use X instead of Y." Builds ingredient trust — the #1 purchase driver for this audience.
25% of content
🪞
Real Skin
UGC amplification, unfiltered before/after, community spotlights. Indian skin tones, real lighting. Counters the polished-ad distrust. Most likely to convert.
25% of content
💬
The Conversation
Hot takes on skincare myths, polls, X threads, replies to trending discourse. "SPF is not just for summer." Builds brand personality, earns followers beyond existing audience.
15% of content

KPIs & Measurement Framework

Success in month one is not follower growth — it's content quality signals. These tell us the strategy is working before numbers scale.

Instagram · Primary KPIs
Save rate >5% per post
Saves = intent. The algorithm reads saves as "this content has lasting value" and rewards it with extended reach. Target: 1 in 20 viewers saves.
Reel watch-through >60%
Tells us the hook is working AND the content holds attention. Below 40% = re-examine first 2 seconds.
Story reply rate >3%
Replies signal real engagement — people are talking to the brand, not just watching it.
X (Twitter) · Primary KPIs
Quote-tweet rate >2%
QTs are X's most powerful amplification. A 2% QT rate on a 1K impression tweet = 20 people bringing it to new audiences. Target this over retweets.
Follower growth +15%/month
Threads and hot takes should attract skincare-interested followers, not just brand loyalists. Track new follower topic interests.
Thread engagement >4%
Threads that educate should earn above-average engagement. Below 2% = topic or framing needs rethinking.
Business · 30-Day Targets
Link-in-bio clicks +20%
Proxy for purchase intent. Story polls → product recs → link taps is the funnel. Track which content type drives most clicks.
UGC submissions: 10+ pieces
Community repost strategy should activate existing customers. 10 organic UGC pieces in 30 days = a healthy community signal.
Brand mention growth +30%
X hot takes and IG conversation content should generate unprompted brand mentions. Track via social listening weekly.
